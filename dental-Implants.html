Dental Implants

Dental implants are an artificial way to fix a tooth root to hold a replacement tooth or bridge. Our dental implants are a perfect choice for people in Kolkatta and anywhere in the world whose tooth or teeth have been lost because of periodontal disease, due to injury or some other accident.

Our dental clinic in Kolkatta specializes in all the basic & latest dental procedures for dental implants, to provide excellent fixed teeth to all out patients. our dental clinic in Kolkatta maintains the highest quality controls in providing Dental Implants. Our dentist are trained and strive to provide the best possible dental care to our patients.

Our Dental implants are so natural to look at and feel that you may never remember that you ever lost a tooth.

The dental implants used by us are of the highest grade and best Quality to offer extra durability, aesthetics and longevity.

Advantages of Dental Implants

Dental implants look and feel just like your natural teeth! Since dental implants fuse into the structure of your bone, they prevent any kind of bone loss or gum recession.

Dental implants don't compromise on the quality of your neighbouring teeth as the neighbouring teeth are not used to support the dental implant.

Dental implants have a very high success rate and are considered as the best option for replacing lost tooth or teeth throughout the world.
